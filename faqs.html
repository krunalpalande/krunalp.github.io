FAQs.

Furiously asked questions about the courses.

1. How are the courses conducted?

All courses are conducted live on Zoom. Each course includes four 90-minute webinars that combine lectures, demonstrations, and interactive exercises.

2. How do I enrol in a course?

Simply fill out the enrolment form on the website. You’ll receive an email with payment details and your Zoom access link before the session begins.

3. Can I book a private session for my team or class?

Yes. If you have a group of participants, you can book a dedicated session. This allows us to tailor the discussions and examples to your specific context.

4. Will recordings be available?

Recordings are not shared publicly, but participants may receive short recap materials or references after each session.

5. Do I need any specific software or tools?

No special software is required. You’ll only need a stable internet connection and a laptop or desktop to participate fully in the exercises and discussions.

6. What if I miss a session?

If you miss a live session, you’ll still receive the key materials, references, and assignments covered that day. You can also attend the next available run of the course.

7. How often do new sessions open?

New sessions are announced regularly. You can check the Homepage/LinkedIn/Discord server for upcoming dates.

8. Can I get a certificate after completing the course?

No, this is not a certification course, but you can take away a lot of learning.

Course Philosophy

9. Do I need prior design experience to join?

No prior design training is required. The courses are designed to be accessible to beginners but also valuable to professionals looking to strengthen their design fundamentals.

10. What makes these courses different from typical design tutorials?

These are based on first principles. These are not tutorial. They focus on thinking more than skills.

11. Will these courses help me in my professional work?

Yes.

12. Is there a community after the course?

We have a Discord
