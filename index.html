About

Data + Design + Storytelling

I combine design, information, and UX to create compelling and memorable information experiences. My mission is to demystify complex issues by transforming data into intuitive and interactive designs that inform and engage.

By blending creativity with analytical rigour, I can help organisations and individuals to communicate effectively, fostering understanding and driving meaningful action.

For the past decade, I’ve been working in world of data visualisation, storytelling and information design. This is a creative space where I make complex things easy to understand. If you have data, information or stories that need bringing to life, I can help.

Trainings

With a blend of practical experience and theoretical knowledge, I’m passionate about teaching the art and science of information design and data visualisation. My training sessions are designed to change how you view and present data, making your visualizations clear, impactful, and engaging.

I provide flexible training options, from concise one-hour workshops to comprehensive full-day sessions, tailored to your needs. As a Data Design faculty member at NMIMS School of Design, Mumbai, I bring academic rigour and industry insights to every program. My training emphasizes the core principles of effective data visualization, applicable across all tools. We explore how to harness the human brain's ability to identify patterns and trends quickly. Each session is filled with real-world examples, ensuring you can apply these skills immediately in your work.
